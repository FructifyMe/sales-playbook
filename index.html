Go-to-Market Strategy

This visual timeline outlines the recommended phased outreach strategy. Following this structured approach will help build momentum, secure flagship logos early, and use that success to penetrate subsequent market segments effectively.

Phase 1 (Months 1-3)

Exclusive Focus on Tier 1 Targets

Concentrate all effort on Tier 1. Use personalized outreach from the dossiers to secure 2-3 flagship logos. This motion is consultative, positioning Nova as the next evolution for their mature wellness strategies.

Phase 2 (Months 4-6)

Expansion to Tier 2 Targets

Expand focus to Tier 2. Use Tier 1 wins as case studies. The motion is more evangelistic, educating on the ROI of wellness and positioning Nova as the ideal foundational investment.

Phase 3 (Ongoing)

Nurture Tier 3 & Inbound Interest

Engage Tier 3 innovation leaders with a long-term "thought leadership" approach on professional networks. Position Nova as a competitive advantage for attracting elite, tech-forward talent.
